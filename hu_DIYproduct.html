How To DIY a Product

Skip to the form below.

Designing a product especially for your decor or item is easy. I will be in touch with you throughout the process. Read through the steps below. Fill out the form. Send the form in. I will contact you by email within 48 hours (typically 24 hours) to answer questions, send examples, or make suggestions.

Shipping

There is no extra charge for US Priority Mail. If other shipping carriers or faster shipping times are needed, we will finalize those details and fee if applicable prior to order placement. I want you to know your price and be comfortable with the arrangements before we start!

DIY Steps

Step 1 - Select the item you want to cover.

Step 2 - Measure the item carefully. I need the item's actual measurements. Height, width and depth. If the product is irregularly shaped, see Step 3 below.

Step 3 - Irregular shapes can pose special problems. Send me an email and I will walk you through measuring this type of item.

Step 4 - Choose a fabric or color scheme and extras. Do you want pockets? Would it look great with fringe? Do you prefer to up-cycle denim? If you are stuck, send a picture of the room where the item lives, and I will send you a few samples to choose from.

Step 5 - Armed with your measurements and ideas, fill out the form below. Required information is marked with an asterisk.
