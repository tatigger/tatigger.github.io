Using Rail Around

Using Rail Around is easy! Find the Valley Metro Rail stop you are interested in on either the Stop Map or in the Stop List. Click on the link to the stop to view the details page. The details page lists the businesses within approximately 1 block of the stop by direction.

How to Buy a Ticket

Valley Metro fares can be bought in person from a kiosk or online. Tickets purchased online are mailed and cannot be downloaded or printed. Reduced fares are available for ages 6-18 and 65+, for people with disabilities, and for people with Medicare. Discounted ASU student and employee passes are also available, call (480) 965-1072 for more information.
