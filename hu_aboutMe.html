About Me

Terri Lyman lives in Northern Arizona. She enjoys the outdoors, kitchen gardening, and crafting. One of her hobbies is observing, tagging and occasionally raising monarch butterflies. She is interested in web design, computer programming, and health care as well. A mother and grandmother, she wants to encourage curiosity and a spirit of adventure in those she meets.

Terri built and uses an insulin dosing device that allows a Medtronic insulin pump, Dexcom Continuous Sensor, and an Edison micro-computer to automatically adjust insulin doses. You can learn more about this at OpenAPS, Loop, Nightscout and Tidepool.

Terri's CrockPot Oriental Chicken

Ingredients
1 bottle Asian Salad Dressing
1 small pack Organic Carrots
1 small Onion, diced
1 pound Chicken, boneless

Add all ingredients to crockpot. Cook on low for at least 6 hours. Remove and shred chicken, then return to sauce. I use this chicken with sauce on rice, gluten free noodles or polenta. I also use the shredded chicken without sauce in chicken salad or as tortilla filling.

I prefer to use chicken thighs or a combination of breast and thighs. I prefer asian flavors in the salad dressing, like ginger, tamari, and sesame. If I need extra liquid for the crockpot, I add chicken stock, balsamic vinegar, or wine.
