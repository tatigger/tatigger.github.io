Crafting

I love to do things that show results pretty quickly. A finished project is a beautiful thing. Crochet is like that for me, when the project is a reasonable size. The image above is part of a baby blanket I made. It was fun and quick, and turned out really well. You can find the free pattern on the Red Heart Yarn webpage.

I found the pattern for this astronaut helmet on Pinterest. I thought it was adorable for my 2-year-old granddaughter (her nursery theme is outer space). I bought a NASA patch to use on the side. I also used a grey yarn with metallic thread for the edges. It was a quick easy project, and my granddaughter was able to "ride her bike to Mars to get apples"!
