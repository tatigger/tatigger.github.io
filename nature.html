Nature

Getting outside to enjoy the world around me does several things. It improves my attitude and lifts my spirits, making the every day challenges feel less stressful. I have to get up and move to make it outside, and this helps me increase my activity. I feel that every little bit helps. I go outside several times a day to watch the hummingbirds, check their feeders, look at the garden, and watch for butterflies. As an added bonus, my dogs think this activity is all about them, and they are happier about life too!
