Welcome!

You are probably here because diabetes is part of your life (or the life of someone you care about), but you don't want it to be your whole life. There are many ways to enjoy life and still live well with diabetes. You will find some of the ways that I try to do that here.

Tell me a little bit about yourself in the survey below. My ideas about food and what I can eat have changed over my years with diabetes. I have had times when I have been sensitive to meat, dairy, and currently gluten. Now I eat fairly low carb, with only about 25 grams of carbohydrate or 1 1/2 bread exchanges per meal. That is not too hard since I can eat meat and dairy right now, but not foods that contain gluten. (Let me tell you, I miss french bread!!!)
