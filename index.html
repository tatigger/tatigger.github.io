Welcome!

You are probably here because diabetes is part of your life (or the life of someone you care about), but you don't want it to be your whole life. There are many ways to enjoy life and still live well with diabetes. You will find some of the ways that I try to do that here.

Tell me a little bit about yourself in the survey below. It is probably a good idea to be aware of what we are drinking each day. Do you get too much caffeine? Are you drinking enough water to stay hydrated? Do you drink carbonated or alcoholic containing beverages? There is no right or wrong answer, this is just a quick assessment to make ourselves aware of what we are drinking. Then we have information to use as we consider what tomorrow will be like.
